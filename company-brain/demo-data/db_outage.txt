=== Postmortem doc — created Friday April 24 by marcus.holt ===

## Summary
At 02:14 UTC Tuesday April 21, 2026, api-prod began returning elevated 5xx and severe latency on /api/v1/projects. Root cause: missing index on tasks.project_id following a botched migration in v3.4.2. Sarah Chen (on-call) detected at 02:14, diagnosed by 02:24, rolled back at 02:32, fully resolved at 02:48. Total customer-impacting time: 48 minutes. Total incident lifecycle (page → all-clear): 94 minutes.

## Customer impact
~2,200 workspaces affected. Four enterprise accounts saw failed page loads. SLA status: still within 99.9% monthly (down to 99.97%), no automatic credits owed. Goodwill credit decision deferred to CS lead per refund policy.

## Five whys
1. Why did /projects fail? Sequential scan on 4.2M-row tasks table per request.
2. Why was there no index? Migration in v3.4.2 dropped the old single-column index and was supposed to add a composite (workspace_id, project_id), but the CONCURRENTLY ADD INDEX silently failed.
3. Why did it silently fail? CI doesn't validate that ADD INDEX CONCURRENTLY actually completed; we trusted exit codes.
4. Why did we ship with that gap? No load test against prod-shaped data — staging hits a tiny dataset where seq scans are fine.
5. Why no load test? Backlog. We've talked about it for 6 months.
